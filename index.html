Ending Hunger in Imo State, Nigeria

Join our mission to achieve Sustainable Development Goal 2: Zero Hunger

Our Mission

At Ibe Uche Helping Hand Foundation, we are committed to eradicating hunger and malnutrition in Imo State through sustainable agricultural programs, food distribution initiatives, and nutrition education.

Working Towards SDG 2: Zero Hunger

We align our efforts with the United Nations Sustainable Development Goal 2 to end hunger, achieve food security and improved nutrition, and promote sustainable agriculture by 2030.
